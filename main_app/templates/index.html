About CEIT

Join us at Gyan Vihar's CEIT department and embark on a journey of discovery, innovation, and excellence in the realm of computer engineering and information technology.

CEIT Department at Gyan Vihar University

The Department of Computer Engineering and Information Technology (CEIT) at Gyan Vihar University stands as a beacon of excellence in the realm of technological education. With a commitment to fostering innovation and research, CEIT offers comprehensive programs that blend theoretical knowledge with practical insights, preparing students for the dynamic tech industry.

Equipped with state-of-the-art infrastructure and led by a team of distinguished faculty, the CEIT department ensures that students are exposed to the latest advancements in the field. Strong industry collaborations offer students unique opportunities for internships and placements, making them sought-after professionals in the global tech arena.

Why Choose CEIT at Gyan Vihar?

  • Our department boasts modern labs, research centers, and classrooms equipped with the latest technology to facilitate hands-on learning.
  • With strong ties to the tech industry, students benefit from internships, workshops, and placement opportunities with leading tech companies.
  • Learn from a team of experienced professors and industry experts who bring a wealth of knowledge and expertise to the classroom.
